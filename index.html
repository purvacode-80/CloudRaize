General FAQs

Everything you need to know

What is the AWS Solutions Architect Micro Degree, and who is it designed for?

The AWS Solutions Architect Micro Degree is an online, instructor-led LIVE program designed for individuals aiming to become proficient AWS Solutions Architects. It's suitable for beginners and those with some programming experience.

Micro Degree course is live or recorded?

The Micro Degree course is an online LIVE course. Prior to the start, you'll receive preparatory material in the form of recorded content accessible on the platform.

In what language will the course be taught?

The course will be conducted in English by the instructors.

How do I access course details and learning materials after registration?

After registration, you’ll receive a confirmation email with login details and a PDF guide for accessing live sessions and materials on the Edyoda Classroom platform.

Do I need to have prior programming experience to enroll?

No prior programming experience is required. The course is suitable for beginners and those with some programming background.

Who are the instructors, and what is their experience?

Our instructors are industry experts with at least 4 years of technical and teaching experience. They bring both practical knowledge and industry insights.

What is the cost of the course, and are there any discounts available?

The course is currently available for ₹1450 + GST (discounted from ₹14,500). Keep an eye on our website for additional promotions.

Will there be assignments, assessments, or a final project in the course?

Yes, the course includes assignments, quizzes, and a final project to reinforce your learning and assess proficiency.

Can I interact with instructors and fellow students during the course?

Yes, there are discussion forums and live Q&A sessions to foster a supportive learning community.
